Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Heidi Astrid Aberdein, and I'm excited to share my journey in front-end web development and digital design with you.

My academic background includes an honors degree in psychology. As a developer, I am skilled in CSS, HTML, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and WordPress, ensuring that the websites I build are not only functional but also visually engaging.

On the design front, I'm proficient in tools like Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects, allowing me to transform concepts into captivating visuals.

With one year of experience as both a developer and designer, I've had the privilege of working with major corporations such as SPAR2U and the National Business Initiative. I've contributed to the SPAR2U app by designing eye-catching icons and improved the SPAR2U's website's functionality through my development skills, as well as implemented API integrations to their website.
